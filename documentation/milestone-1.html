Purpose

This website will be a gallery to display handcrafted chainmail jewelry and adornments, including information about the pieces to share the crafting process involved.

Audience

The intended audience is anyone looking to find hand-crafted jewelry, either looking to buy a piece for themselves, or to see reference of what kind of work can be done within the chainmail style. Will be designed to appeal to other creators, or professionals in related fields such as costume design and fashion. Target audience could include: other creators, models and photographers, designers, and artists.

Goal

The primary goal of this website is to provide users with a way to contact me with questions and commission requests for handcrafted chainmail jewelry. The secondary goal is to have a professionally structured and designed gallery site that presents multiple jewelry pieces with informational sections about them.

Persona for Allison Apertura

Profession:

A professional photographer/costume designer who works with models and creates themed photoshoots. Familiar with photo editing and design software, as well as networking with models and studios.

Technology:

Proficient user, Mac laptop and Iphone. High-end camera and photography equipment. Proficient with photo-editing software and communication networks.

Attitudes and Behavior:

Organized with many different areas and tasks to manage, such as: Networking with models, studios, and fellow photographers, finding shoot locations, marketing, coordinating with models and teams, managing social media, research and assembly of costumes.

Frustrations and Needs:

Looking for unique jewelry that can fit a given theme. Needs demonstrations of enough variety in the pieces to be able to match complex ideas and designs, and fit into other costumes. Frustrated by the saturation and misrepresentation of mass-produced clothes and jewelry.

Goal:

Review sites and collect contact information from artists and hand crafted jewelry that meet a standard for professional presentation, and clearly displays a variety of well crafted products to potentially be used in photoshoots and costume designs.

User Story:

As a user, I need to see high quality examples and presentation of unique pieces so that I can determine if I can commission the artist for designs that fit my ideas and themes.
